The Four Phases

🦶
Movement
Move up to 6 inches
▼

Each of your models can move up to 6 inches in any direction.

You can move each model independently. Models cannot move through other models or off the table.

Tip: Move your models towards enemies or objectives, but think about where they'll be when shooting starts!

🎯
Shooting
Attack at range, up to 8"
▼

Pick a model and a target enemy within 8 inches. Roll three dice:

Hit 4+
→
Wound 4+
→
Save 4+

Hit: Roll a die — score 4 or more to hit.

Wound: If you hit, roll again — score 4 or more to wound.

Save: The defender rolls — 4 or more means the armour holds and nothing happens. Below 4? Remove that model!

💨
Charge
Rush into close combat
▼

Choose a model to charge at an enemy within 12 inches. Roll 2 dice and add them together — if the total reaches the enemy, the charge succeeds and you move right next to them!

If the total isn't enough, the charge fails and the model stays put.

Tip: Charging is exciting but risky — only charge if you really want to get stuck in!

👊
Fight
Swing at close range
▼

Models next to an enemy can fight! The same three rolls apply:

Hit 4+
→
Wound 4+
→
Save 4+

Both players fight — the attacker goes first, then the defender strikes back! Even if a model is killed, it still gets to fight before it's removed.
